he George Brown College of Applied Arts and Technology is a public, fully accredited college of applied arts and technology with three campuses in downtown Toronto (Ontario, Canada). Like many other colleges in Ontario, George Brown College was chartered in 1966 by the government of Ontario and opened the next year.
Located in Toronto, Canada, George Brown College (GBC) is a well-known public college offering a range of programs in technology, business, and hospitality.
George Brown offers more than 170 full-time programs in art and design, business, community services, early childhood education, construction and engineering technologies, health sciences, hospitality and culinary arts, preparatory studies, as well as specialized programs and services for recent immigrants and international students.
The college offers diploma programs, advanced diploma programs, as well as degree programs, two in conjunction with Toronto Metropolitan University.
GBC emphasizes practical, hands-on learning through industry projects and real-world scenarios.
Industry Connections: The college has strong ties with various industries, providing students with networking and career opportunities
Diverse Student Community: GBC has a multicultural student body, offering a vibrant and inclusive learning environment.
Career-Focused Programs: Most programs, including DevOps, are designed to meet industry demands and help students secure jobs after graduation.
 Students get hands-on experience with tools like AWS, Git, and scripting  (Bash, Python) to automate workflows.
